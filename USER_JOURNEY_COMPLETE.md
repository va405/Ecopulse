# 🌍 Complete User Journey - EcoPulse

## 📋 Problem Statement Recap

**Core Challenge:** Individuals lack awareness of their personal carbon footprint and don't know how to take effective action to reduce environmental impact.

**Solution Goals:**
1. ✅ **Carbon Footprint Awareness** - Help users understand their emissions
2. ✅ **Time-Based Impact Tracking** - Show progress over time
3. ✅ **Actionable Reduction Strategies** - Provide concrete steps
4. ✅ **Personalized AI Recommendations** - Tailor advice to user behavior
